Game Title: Word Hunt Challenge Description: "Guess the Word - " is a fun and engaging word guessing game where players must guess a hidden word within a limited number of tries and time.
Are you up for the challenge ?

Objective: Your goal in this game is to guess the hidden word correctly before you run out of attempts and time. With each incorrect guess you no. of guess will decrease , But be cautious, as you only have a limited number of tries!

Rules:
The game will select a random word, which is represented by a series of boxes (one for each letter), to hide the word's identity.
Players take turns guessing letters, one at a time.
If your guessed letter is part of the hidden word, it will be revealed in its corresponding position(s). If not, you'll lose one of your limited attempts.

Hints:
The hint will provide a brief clue or category related to the hidden word. Use this information to make educated guesses.

Winning: If you correctly guess the hidden word within the allowed number of incorrect attempts, you win the game!
Losing: you lose the game. if you runout of guess and time. Enjoy the game, and have fun challenging your word-guessing skills!
